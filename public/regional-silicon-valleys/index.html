DETAILS

With increasing pressure to innovate and create jobs, most local governments and large corporates are busy building ecosystems of entrepreneurs and innovators. Many launch various support initiatives and programmes. But entrepreneurs struggle to find the right support among uncoordinated people, activities and websites. As a result many organisations to demonstrate momentum and critical mass. This is where the ecosystem management app can help.

Startups get visibility of their company in the community, and the ability to contact investors and mentors.

Investors can use the platform to track start-ups and their progress.

Community managers get visibility for their programs and events and integrated tools to run competitions, mentor programs, accelerators and incubator programs. In addition they can get unprecedented data on their ecosystem size, activities and the result of their activities
